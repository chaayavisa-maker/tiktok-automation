🔒 Privacy Policy

TikTok Content Automation Platform

Privacy First: We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring secure handling of your data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary for our TikTok content automation service:

Data Type Purpose Source
TikTok Authorization Tokens Posting content to your account TikTok OAuth
Basic Account Info Username, posting permissions TikTok API
Content Data Generated scripts, videos, schedules Our AI systems
Usage Analytics Service performance, optimization Service logs

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Content Creation: Generating scripts, videos, and thumbnails
  • Automated Posting: Publishing content to your TikTok account
  • Trend Research: Analyzing trends for content optimization
  • Service Optimization: Improving automation performance
  • User Support: Providing technical assistance when needed

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT share, sell, or distribute your personal information to third parties, except:

  • TikTok API: Only for posting content (official API integration)
  • AI Services: Replicate AI for video generation (no personal data shared)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Service Providers: Essential infrastructure partners with strict agreements
Security Measures: All third-party integrations use secure APIs and follow industry best practices for data protection.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Token Security: OAuth tokens stored with encryption at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Cloud services with enterprise-grade security

5. Data Retention

We retain data based on operational necessity:

  • Authorization Tokens: Until you revoke access or delete account
  • Generated Content: 30 days for optimization purposes
  • Analytics Data: 90 days in aggregated, anonymized form
  • Support Logs: 1 year for troubleshooting and improvements

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about data we have collected
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Revocation: Revoke access permissions at any time
  • Portability: Request export of your data

7. TikTok Integration Privacy

Regarding our TikTok integration:

  • Official APIs Only: We use TikTok's official Content Posting API
  • OAuth 2.0: Secure authorization without storing passwords
  • Limited Scope: Only posting permissions, no personal data access
  • User Control: You can revoke access anytime in TikTok settings
  • TikTok's Privacy: Subject to TikTok's own privacy policy

8. Cookie Policy

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Authentication: Temporary session management
  • No Tracking: We do not use tracking or advertising cookies
  • Cookie Control: You can disable cookies in browser settings

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Parents can contact us to request data deletion
  • We comply with COPPA and similar regulations
  • Age verification through TikTok's own systems

10. International Data Transfers

If you're outside the United States:

  • Data may be processed in the US or other countries
  • We ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses
  • EU users: We comply with GDPR requirements
  • Data protection laws of your country may apply

11. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  • We will notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Authorities will be notified as required by law
  • We will provide clear information about the incident
  • Steps to protect your account will be communicated

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy:

  • Users will be notified of material changes
  • Updated policy will be posted on our website
  • Continued use implies acceptance of changes
  • You can review policy changes history

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Privacy Email: privacy@tiktok-automation.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@tiktok-automation.com
  • General Support: support@tiktok-automation.com
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours
  • Mailing Address: Available upon request
